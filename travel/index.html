What is a travel journal? This is where I hope to write about places I've been & what I learned from being there. I think a good travel journal entry should include the following elements:

  • A log of destinations and meaningful experiences & locations
  • Recommendations and suggestions for prospective travelers
  • Reflections and lessons learned from the time spent there
